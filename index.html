We create immersion with a Haitian heartbeat

Founded in Port-au-Prince, Zantray VR combines interactive storytelling, 3D artistry, and human-centered design to translate the vibrancy of Haitian culture into immersive journeys. Our name, inspired by the Creole word for "center," reflects our belief that technology should keep people at the center of every experience.

Education & workforce

Prepare the next generation with VR training modules for health, construction, and hospitality programs delivered in Haitian Creole and French.

Tourism & culture

Create unforgettable previews of heritage sites from Citadelle Laferrière to Jacmel Carnival through sensory-rich storytelling.

Disaster readiness

Run realistic simulations that help communities and first responders practice coordinated responses before storms strike.
